Game Rules


To start the game, players must agree on a winning score, and whoever reaches the winning score first wins the game.

At each turn, the player has two options: continue to roll the dice or to hold (and thus accumulate) current score.

If any of the following occurs during a turn, current player loses all current score, and the game is passed on to the next player:

  • If either dice rolls a 1

  • If two 6's are rolled in a row
